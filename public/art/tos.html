WEREDYKE COMMISSION T.O.S


Terms apply to anyone who commissions artwork from @weredyke/@dojamutt. Not following T.O.S could result in a blacklist from commissioning artwork in the future. (EDITED 10/30/2024)

GENERAL TERMS

RIGHTS

Do whatever you want with your art, edit it, repost it. You CANNOT put it on a product to sell.


PRICING

When you consult me for a commission I will tell you how much money in USD to transfer over Paypal, Cashapp or Venmo. If your commission needs a deadline you can pay more for priority.


CLIENT'S DUTY

You must send me visual references for POSES, CHARACTERS, OUTFITS, and optionally anything else that helps me better depict your character. I will not design a character for you.


DRAWING PROCESS

I will make a rough sketch of the pose(s), once you're satisfied I will continue your drawing. I will send updates throughout but you may ask for more. My art takes me 1-2 months to complete. My art is a DIGITAL good, it will be sent to you over discord or email.
